About Sophia

Sophia is a Michigan native, turned Indiana transplant. As a sophmore in college, she worked with the Michigan Economic Development Corporation - the creators of the Pure Michigan campaign. After completing a degree in Business Communications from Albion College, she worked for a venture capital firm in Birmingham, MI. One year later, this Michigan enthusist found herself working at a law firm in Indianapolis. Now, she is a front-end engineering student at The Iron Yard in Indy.

Though she lives in Indiana, during the summer you can find her on trips to Mackinac Island or Lake Michigan. Her favorite destinations include Saugatuck (on Lake Michigan), the Detroit Riverfront, and the Sleeping Bear Sand Dunes.
